## Check-in Policy

IndiGo Airlines offers multiple check-in options:
- **Web check-in**: Available 48 hours to 60 minutes before departure at goindigo.in
- **Mobile check-in**: Via IndiGo app, same timeline as web check-in
- **Airport check-in**: Counter opens 3 hours before departure, closes 45 minutes before
- **Self-service kiosks**: Available at major airports, closes 45 minutes before departure

## Baggage Allowance

### Economy Class
- **Cabin baggage**: 7 kg per passenger (1 piece, max 55x35x25 cm)
- **Checked baggage**: 15 kg included in base fare (some routes include 20kg)

### Business Class (IndiGo 6E Prime)
- **Cabin baggage**: 10 kg
- **Checked baggage**: 30 kg included

### Excess Baggage Fees
- Pre-purchase at booking: ₹600 per kg (cheapest option)
- Pre-purchase online before travel: ₹800 per kg
- At airport check-in counter: ₹1,000 per kg

## Cancellation & Refund Policy

- **Cancel 30+ days before**: Full refund minus ₹3,500 cancellation fee
- **Cancel 15-29 days before**: 75% refund
- **Cancel 0-14 days before**: 50% refund
- **No-show**: No refund (fare is forfeited)
- Refunds processed in 5-7 business days to original payment method

## Seat Selection

- **Free seats**: Middle seats available at no charge during web check-in
- **Preferred seats (extra legroom)**: ₹500–₹1,500 depending on route
- **Hot Seats (front rows)**: ₹700–₹2,000
- Seat selection opens 48 hours before departure

## Meals

- IndiGo does not offer complimentary meals on domestic flights
- Meals must be pre-ordered at booking or via Manage Booking section
- **Meal pre-booking**: ₹150–₹600 depending on meal type
- Airport food purchases available but more expensive

## Special Assistance

- Wheelchair assistance: Request at least 48 hours before flight (free of charge)
- Unaccompanied Minor service: Available for children 5-12 years, fee applies
- Medical clearance: Required for passengers with serious medical conditions
- Pet travel: Not permitted in cabin; cargo transport available for select routes

## IndiGo 6E Rewards

- Earn 6E points on every IndiGo flight
- 1 6E point = ₹0.50 redemption value
- Points valid for 1 year from last transaction
- Redeem points against base fare only

## Flight Status & Delays

- Flight status available on goindigo.in, app, or call 0124-6173838
- SMS alerts sent to registered mobile for delays > 30 minutes
- For delays > 3 hours: Complimentary meal voucher provided at airport

## Refund for Cancelled Flights (by Airline)

- If IndiGo cancels your flight: Full refund within 7 business days
- Alternative flight offered at no extra charge
- Compensation as per DGCA guidelines applies

## Baggage Claim & Lost Luggage

- Collect baggage at designated carousel shown on arrival screens
- File PIR (Property Irregularity Report) within 24 hours for lost baggage
- Delayed baggage: Delivered to your address within 24-48 hours
- Lost baggage claim: Up to ₹20,000 compensation after investigation

## International Travel

- Valid passport required with minimum 6 months validity
- Visa responsibility of passenger — IndiGo not liable for visa issues
- International check-in closes 60 minutes before departure
- Additional baggage allowances for international routes (check booking)
